    • A Revival of Deceit...

    Within the Smash world...a deceptive game of adventure begins.

    Ten fighters are thrust into a difficult game of intelligence, mystery, and betrayal. Nine of them must work together to complete a series of tasks...but one is secretly against them: the Mole. He or she is blending among the others and slowly destroying their game, being the secret beytrayer of ruin and destruction. And because of this...no one can be trusted.

    But who is it?




    One by one, the players are eliminated based on their lack of knowledge about the deceiver. But the sole player to survive to the end of the game and identity the hidden saboteur among them wins the game.

    Can YOU figure out who the Mole is?
